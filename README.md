Acquisition Gap: Total installs reached 1.98M (+9.0%), but the Sign-up Rate dropped by 38.8%, indicating a bottleneck in the registration funnel.

Platform Performance: Android is the leading platform for transactions with 271,639, compared to 199,456 on iOS.


Regional Leaders: The US dominates transaction volume, significantly outperforming EMEA, Canada, APAC, and LATAM.
